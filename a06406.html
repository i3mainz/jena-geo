Detailed Description

A view over another image with the origin relocated to a new position.

Only the pixel coordinates are changed; the tile indices stay the same. However the image view may expose less tiles than the wrapped image. This wrapper does not change image size otherwise than by an integer amount of tiles.

Author
Johann Sorel (Geomatys)
Martin Desruisseaux (Geomatys)
